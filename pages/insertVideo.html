Introduction to Economics 120

Module 1: Awareness and Orientation

Welcome to your first section of the Economics 120 content in the IDEA program.

You know a little bit about the mindset of an entrepreneur—so how does an Economist think? Even though the disciplines of economics and entrepreneurship are related, it seems that their perspective is different. An entrepreneur’s passion for an idea will get a business started, but it is an understanding of basic economic principles that will help sustain and scale a business.

Have a listen to hear what some Economists at Simon Fraser University have to say about the study of economics.

In this module, you will:

  • Define key concepts that define the study of economics
