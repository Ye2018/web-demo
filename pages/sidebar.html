An entrepreneur is a professional problem solver. Developing an entrepreneurial mindset is an important step towards preparing yourself for living and working in the 21st Century.

Throughout this program you will develop skills that you can apply to anything that you do.

On successful completion of this module, you will be able to:

  • Identify your interests, passions and personality traits.
  • Define key terms associated with entrepreneurship.
  • Identify the unique characteristics of an entrepreneur.
  • Identify and connect with local businesses in your community.
  • Interview an entrepreneur using established effective interviewing techniques.
  • Create SMART objectives to help you effectively monitor your progress.
