Connecting With Your Community

Module 1: Awareness and Orientation

New Brunswick communities have been found to be amongst the most supportive of youth entrepreneurship in all of Canada.

Successful entrepreneurship leverages the strengths of a community to build new ideas, and luckily, communities in New Brunswick have growing networks that encourage and support entrepreneurship.

The government of New Brunswick is very concerned about young people moving away from NB and so they have begun encouraging the growth of engaged, fulfilled workers through a number of initiatives:

  • Free tuition programs
  • Special financial grants
  • Interest free loans and internships
  • Free training programs for workers that sign a contract to stay in NB for at least 3 years after graduation
  • Sponsored internships to allow young people get experience in their chosen field of work
  • High school markets, business competitions, internships and summer projects sponsored by communities in support of social entrepreneurship
  • Organizations such as the IDEA Centre that provides entrepreneurial opportunities to high school students
